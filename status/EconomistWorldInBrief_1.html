World in brief at 2024.10.21

2024-10-21 00:00:00

Israel said it hit around 300 targets in Lebanon in the past 24 hours. The barrage was directed at Hizbullah’s economic “system and strongholds”, including the al-Qard al-Hassan Association, which is believed to be managing Hizbullah’s finances. The group has more than 30 branches across Lebanon. The UN’s human rights office condemned the attacks, which it says caused “another wave of displacement” and were not legitimate targets under international humanitarian law.

For the first time in two months Donald Trump has taken the lead in our US election model, which now gives him a 54 in 100 chance of winning the election. Just as national polls showed many undecided and third-party voters choosing Kamala Harris in August, some of the remaining ones—possibly Republican leaners all along—have recently lined up behind Mr Trump.
