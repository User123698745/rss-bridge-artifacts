    • High Quality Weapon Icon Tool

    2024-10-14 06:02:25

    by: heydo6


    High Quality Weapon Icon Tool

    Things you'll need...
    -  This mod
    - UltraCam
    - Image editing software

    Very simple mod which changes the textures of "FldObj_WoodStorage"
    Works with all versions and requires no RSTB edits.
    Simply drop the mod in an emulator and toggle on/off when needed.
    If you're familiar with image editing software you can take a picture and
    and create a high quality icon in a few minutes.

    Getting Started
    - Install mod and UltraCam on emulator
    - Drop the desired weapon on the wood storage platform
    - Toggle on UltraCam, line up camera on weapon
    - Adjust time of day, lighting with UltraCam  ( - & + ) on controller
    - Take a few pictures of weapon or item

    Note: Weapons don't always line up at 45 degree, take the time to get the angle correct.

    Image software
    - Open your image in Photoshop
    - Crop image of weapon similar to vanilla Icons
    - Resize your cropped image to 288x288
    - Open Magic Wand tool and outline the edge of your weapon precisely
    - Copy the image
    - Open Eraser Tool and erase entire image
    - Paste image and move to desired position
    or
    - Have a blank image 288x288 and paste there
    - Save as png

    The white textured background makes creating a precise outline with Magic Wand tool quick and easy.

    Default texture is white, I've also included a black texture for white weapons, items.
    If you don't understand any of these terms try a quick Google search.
    Example... "How to use Magic Wand with Photoshop"

    This is out of the scope of this mod but quick run down of the steps in Switch Toolbox.
    Replacing image with Switch Toolbox
    - Copy a donor Icon from your game dump
    - Rename donor Icon, if needed
    - Drop file into Switch ToolBox
    - Rename all internal names, if needed
    - Right click and replace image with the png we created
    - Save

    Sorry, didn't want this to turn into a guide just quickly run through the steps.
    I've been using this method a while without much downside.
    Quick and quality Icons even with complicated models.
    Hope you enjoy <3

    • Armored Tunic of Memories

    2024-10-07 08:01:30

    by: hulmerist


    This mod adds some basic armor to the Tunic of Memories.

    Isn't it a bit strange that the tunic which Link wore throughout most of Breath of the Wild has practically no defensive capability? Aside from a single gauntlet on his right arm, the Tunic of Memories is pretty much just a shirt. No wonder it got completely destroyed during the Calamity.

    This mod aims to fix that by giving the Tunic of Memories some extra armor. The Tunic of Memories now has new bracers and chest armor to go along with its gauntlet. Now, it looks more like an outfit fit for adventuring rather than ceremonial garb.

    This mod uses models and textures from the Hylian Tunic, so any mod that recolors the chest armor or bracers of the Hylian Tunic will also affect this mod. This also means that this mod is compatible with any mods that recolor or retexture the Tunic of Memories (Green Tunic of Memories shown in the screenshots as an example) since the textures for the armor are independent from the actual tunic.
