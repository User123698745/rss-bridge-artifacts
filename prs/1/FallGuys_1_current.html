Step Up Your Game: Fall Guys Ranked Knockout is Here!

2025-04-01 04:00:00

Knockout the competition, climb up the Ranks, and earn exclusive rewards!

(Video: https://youtu.be/Q9tMb7l-z4Q)

Fall Guys has entered the competitive arena, and it’s a knockout! Or, should we say it’s a Ranked Knockout — an all-new mode just added to Fall Guys that introduces a skill-based progression system, exciting challenges, and a whole bunch of rewards. 

Jump in now and show all those other Beans what you’re made of.

What is Ranked Knockout?

Think you’re the biggest and baddest Bean around? Ranked Knockout is your chance to prove it! This new mode functions just like the Knockout you know and love, but with the addition of eight competitive ranks — Rookie, Contender, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Ace, Star, and Superstar — each of which is divided into Sub Ranks:
 

  • Rookie (Rookie 1-3)
  • Contender (Contender 1-3)
  • Bronze (Bronze 1-3)
  • Silver (Silver 1-3)
  • Gold (Gold 1-5)
  • Ace (Ace 1-5)
  • Star (Star 1-5)
  • Superstar (Just 1 rank — for the best of the best!)

All players will start as a Rookie, and must earn points to be promoted to a higher Rank. Points are earned based on your performance in Ranked Knockout matches — the closer you get to the Final, the more points you’ll receive! But bean-ware, you can also lose points (and eventually drop Ranks) for noob-like antics. Don’t act surprised, this is Fall Guys after all…

Ranked Knockout is divided into eight-week-long Competitive Cycles. At the end of each Cycle, all players receive Rewards based on their highest attained Rank.

How Ranked Knockout Works

Scoring System
While at one of the initial four Ranks — Rookie, Contender, Bronze, or Silver — it’s impossible to fall through the Ranks, even if you yeet yourself off the Map one hundred times in a row (although we wouldn’t recommend that).

The last four Ranks — Gold, Ace, Star, or Superstar — are where the going gets tough, and the Beans get down to business. You’ll have points deducted each time you fail to make it through the initial rounds of each match and will eventually be demoted after repeated mishaps.

In the event you are demoted (don’t worry, we all have off days), you can only drop to a lower Sub Rank, not a full Rank. For example, if you’re at Ace 3, you’ll tumble down to Ace 2 and eventually to Ace 1, but you can’t drop all the way to Gold 3.

Matchmaking
To keep the competition fair, Ranked Knockout's matchmaking system will place you against players as close to your current rank as possible. Regardless of rank, the round pool of available levels remains the same for every player.

Competitive Cycles
At the end of each Competitive Cycle, all players will have their Rank reset to give every Bean another shot at the big time. Each Cycle will also switch up the Level Pool and introduce new Challenges, so brace yourself for unexpected obstacles!

Anti-Cheating Policies
Our team is committed to fighting cheating in Ranked Knockout and will be on the lookout for any suspicious behavior. If you encounter another player in Fall Guys who you believe may be cheating, please report them to us by following this guide.

Rank Up and Rake In Those Rewards!

Becoming a Superstar isn’t just about the bragging rights, it’s about winning some un-bean-lieveable rewards too! Firstly, you’ll be awarded Kudos and Crowns at the end of each Competitive Cycle based on your highest achieved Rank.

If you managed to make it to one of the top four Ranks, you’ll also be awarded a Ranked Emoticon. The appearance of these unique Emoticons, which are exclusive to Ranked Knockout, will change depending on whether you reached Gold, Ace, Star, or Superstar during the Competitive Cycle. Reach Superstar, and your Emoticon will be super-duper shiny! Oooh.

Reach Star or Superstar Rank to earn the ultimate prize — the Supreme Idol Costume! Each Competitive Cycle (every 8 weeks) features a new premium Costume and Ranked Emoticon to unlock, so keep coming back to show off your skills and claim fresh rewards.

Fall into Ranked Knockout!

Whether you’re a seasoned Bean wanting to prove your skills or a relative newcomer looking to explore the competitive scene, Ranked Knockout is designed to give all Fall Guys players the opportunity to demonstrate their prowess. Plus, with new challenges and exclusive rewards up for grabs each Competitive Cycle, there is plenty of reason to keep coming back and climbing the path to becoming a Superstar.

Once you've bounced, bumped, and bumbled your way through Ranked Knockout, make sure to tell us about your experiences on social media! Your feedback will give us valuable insights into how to improve Ranked Knockout in future updates.

Ready to rise to the top? Jump into Ranked Knockout today and show the world you’re the best Bean there has ever been.

    Let’s Explore Together

    With Explore Mode, there’s less waiting and near endless levels to have a chill time with friends.

    New Mode: Explore
    Explore keeps the party going with a non-stop pool of creator made levels for you & your party to dive into! Not feeling the Level? Skip to the next one! Explore has been built with the feedback we’ve seen from Shows like Unexplored Adventures, see below for the features that make up this new Mode.

    No more waiting for others
    We’ve heard you don’t like waiting so this mode allows you to queue up for the next level the moment you cross the finish line.

    Skip levels you don’t enjoy
    Not feeling a Level? We’ve added a Skip function that will let you hop onto the next Level whenever you like.

    Make new friends
    Explore is a 10 player mode with matchmaking so you can meet new players and make new friends.

    Less Stressful
    For courses (formerly called races) we have set timers to 30 minutes to allow you to take the time you need. Since other beans can move onto the next level even if you haven’t finished, you don’t have to worry about holding back other players.

    Earn Rewards
    Had a long play session and need a break? Feel free. Rewards will be waiting for you based on how many Levels you’ve completed. You will also earn progress towards Challenges and the Fame Pass whenever you play Explore.

    Explore Level Selection
    Explore levels will be populated from an ever evolving algorithm of the most liked and newest creator made rounds. It is key for never before rounds to be in Explore so that it feels endless and so we can find the next great level. If you enjoy a level, let it be known with a like. If you load into a level you don’t care for, just hold the skip button and continue to explore. Levels will also automatically update if changes are detected. This means creators can update levels based on player feedback and see their changes go live.

Once pressed you will see a Piggyback request appear above your Bean & when another player accepts you will be able to hop on their back. Piggyback lets two players connect together, with one carrying the other through tricky troubles or just for fun! Once you’re done you can hop off their back whenever you like.

    Easily let other Beans know what you’re thinking with new Social Wheels. With Emoticons, and eight Quick Phrases, you can share how you feel with a flick of the wheel!

    More stickers to decorate your Levels with!

    We’ve made some adjustments to the way the bean reacts to physics to reduce frustrations and deliver a more fun gameplay experience. An example of this is when you’re going fast, land on your feet and then find yourself rolling on the floor.     Your jump height and acceleration has been adjusted also, meaning all jumps will now have more leniency and you will reach top running speed faster. We believe this will make Fall Guys more fun, dynamic and a bit more forgiving

    We want players to have a bit more control to reposition their bean when airborne, and to save themselves from elimination if they find themselves being yeeted through the sky.
