    I HEARD IT’S A MESS THERE TOO

    Dear traveler,
    It’s a mess out here. I heard it’s a mess there too.
    I’ve been home pressing buttons and turning knobs, mostly. The dogs get me out of the house which I am grateful for.
    I wanted to drop these recent experiments off for you. I was trying out some new tools and caught a little wave on my way to wherever I’m headed. The songs feel more transitional than they do a final destination - but I had fun making them.
    I hope things around your way get better soon. Until then - take care of one another.
    Best
    A.R.
